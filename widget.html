Copy this code

Paste this snippet anywhere in your website's HTML. The widget will automatically load with pricing data for your city.

<script src="https://flowerindexdata.com/widget.js"></script> <div class="fid-widget" data-city="phoenix" data-state="az" data-index="valentines"></div>

Customization Options

Choose Your Index

Display pricing for different holidays and occasions.

data-index="valentines"
data-index="mothers-day"
data-index="sympathy"
data-index="christmas"

Set Your Location

Show pricing for any city in our database.

data-city="houston"
data-state="tx"

Dark Mode

Match your site's dark theme.

data-theme="dark"

Compact Size

Smaller widget for sidebars.

data-size="compact"
